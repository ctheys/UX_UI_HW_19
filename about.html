About Me

I am a UX/UI designer passionate about optimising users’ experience. My design process is centred around discovery and early validation. I have a special interest in user research, usability testing, interaction design and programming.

A little more about me?

I traded my very buttery French pastries for a very Melbournian avocado-healthy toastie. Since my sweet childhood, I’ve been tinkering with websites, learning new languages, and immersing myself in the world of design. And during my free time, I try (and sometimes fail) to keep my plants alive.
